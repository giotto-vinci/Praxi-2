About Website

This is my first unassigned test website. Its purpose is to show great art throughout history in a more responsive and free flowing manner. The majority of websites I come across are not as user friendly as I think they could be. From my experience when coming across an art website you need a basic knowledge of some artists, styles, or movements to begin browsing artworks. The plan is to provide scrolling images of great works from history and, based on the user's preference, they can click on pieces they like to learn more about the artist, related artists, styles, and movements.
